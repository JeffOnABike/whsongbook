About

Mission

The West House Songbook is an open-source songbook. Its mission is to provide lyrics and chords to traditional and popular music in a simple and accurate format.

History

This songbook began as a traditional paper collection of trascriptions around 2007 at West House, a vegetarian co-operative at Brown University, thanks largely to the work of John Keller. It was then expanded by Emilie Lygren and Jason Reeder in 2010. The current digital re-imagining began in 2016 and is maintained by Jason Reeder.
